FIFA World Cup 2022: Qatar

In the November-December period of 2022, the biggest festival for every football fan occurred worldwide. This time, the destination was none other than the Gulf country of Qatar for the 22nd edition of the most prestigious prize, the "World Cup." Thirty-six teams fought it out for glory, making it an amazing rollercoaster of emotions. The greatest of all time, Lionel Messi, conquered his final peak, lifting the World Cup after Argentina beat France 4-2 in penalties.
