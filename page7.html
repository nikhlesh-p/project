Not Over Yet

On the 17th of December, football enthusiasts witnessed the culmination of the FIFA World Cup 2022's third-place play-off, a riveting encounter that carried a sense of déjà vu. The two formidable teams, having clashed in their opening Group F game with a scoreless draw, ignited the field with renewed intensity. Croatia's Joško Gvardiol broke the deadlock, seizing an early lead promptly countered by Achraf Dari's swift equalizer just two minutes later. In a narrative of resilience and determination, Mislav Oršić etched his name in history, securing the winning goal for Croatia as the match concluded 2–1. The heartening victory secured the third-place position for Croatia, while Morocco, with a commendable 4th place finish, achieved a historic milestone, marking the best World Cup outcome for any African or Arab nation.
